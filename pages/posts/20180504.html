Research

Last month I made weekly trips to county of a family of mine, Union County. I had been meaning to go and conduct research and I finally made the trip. It has boosted my research immensely. Not only in my knowledge of my family but my research skills. One of the resources I found an abundance on is profiles of certain people. These profiles included descriptions of them, their life, travels, experience and such. A lot of these profiles were of Civil War soldier. This has inspired me to try and build more of these type profiles for other family members.

My trip included visiting cemeteries, Heritage Room, and library for information. My next trip will include visiting the courthouse. I have not visited lately. I am still going through the data I've collected the last few trips. As well as working on the to-do list for the trip. I always go with a plan to have to most productive time there since I cannot visit often.

Along with that I am implementing some of the information speakers have talked about in conferences I have listened to on YouTube about microservices. I am also going to continue with a project I have not published yet to my GitHub which is a DateParser. This project is allowing users to enter multiple types of inputs for dates, regular formats (05 May 1945, 05/05/1945), dates that include approximate dates, between two dates, before/after dates, etc.
